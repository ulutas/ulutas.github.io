vPrompt Mobile Application Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 5, 2025

O. ULUTAS ("We", "Us", "Our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the "vPrompt" mobile application ("Application"). By downloading, installing, accessing, or using the Application, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect information such as your username, email address, and password.
  • User-Generated Content: We collect the keywords you input into the Application and the prompts generated through our AI system based on your keywords.
  • Communication: When you contact us for support or feedback, we collect the information you provide in your communications.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: We collect information about your mobile device, including device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the Application, including app features used, time spent in the app, frequency of use, and crash reports.
  • Technical Data: We collect IP addresses, browser type and version, time zone setting, and other technical information.

1.3 Subscription and Payment Information

  • Payment Processing: Payment transactions are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not directly collect or store your payment card information. We receive transaction confirmations and subscription status information from Apple.
  • Credit System Data: We track your credit balance, credit usage history, and credit acquisition records to manage the in-app credit system.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide, maintain, and improve the Application's functionality, including AI-powered prompt generation services.
  • Account Management: To create and manage your user account, authenticate your identity, and provide customer support.
  • Subscription Management: To process subscriptions, manage billing, track credit usage, and provide access to premium features.
  • Personalization: To customize your experience and improve the relevance of generated prompts based on your usage patterns.
  • Communication: To send you important updates about the Application, respond to your inquiries, and provide customer support.
  • Analytics and Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, identify bugs, improve app performance, and develop new features.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal requests.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the Application, including:

  • AI Service Providers: To process your keywords and generate prompts using artificial intelligence services.
  • Cloud Storage Providers: To store and backup your data securely.
  • Analytics Providers: To analyze app usage and performance (data is anonymized where possible).
  • Customer Support Tools: To provide efficient customer service and technical support.

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others.

3.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the business transaction.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication measures
  • Secure cloud storage infrastructure

Your data is stored on secure servers located in reputable data centers. We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

5. Data Retention

  • Account Data: We retain your account information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.
  • Usage Data: We retain usage data for up to 24 months for analytics and service improvement purposes.
  • User-Generated Content: Your keywords and generated prompts are retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period after account deletion for backup purposes.
  • Credit Information: Credit balance and transaction history are retained for as long as your account is active and for tax and legal compliance purposes thereafter.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Portability: Request a copy of your personal information in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your personal information.
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section below.
