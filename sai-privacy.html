Introduction

This Policy applies to the App offered by SAI ("We", "Us", "Our").

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use the App:

  • **Personal Information:**
    • **Account Information:** Information such as your email address and username when you register for the App.
    • **Payment Information:** Information related to payments for subscriptions or in-app credit purchases, but this information is not stored directly by us. All payment transactions are handled through third-party payment processors like Apple App Store, whose own privacy policies will apply.
  • **Usage Data:**
    • Information about how you use the App (e.g., which features are used, number of videos created, credits spent).
    • Device information (device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers).
    • Log data such as IP address, timestamps, and frequency of access to the App.
    • App crashes and error reports.
  • **User-Generated Content:**
    • Text, images, and other media inputs (prompts) that you provide for video creation.
    • The final videos you create through the App.
  • **Third-Party Data:**
    • We may receive anonymized and/or aggregated data from third-party analytics services like Google Analytics to analyze and improve the App's usage.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the App and its services.
  • To manage your account, process your subscriptions, and credits.
  • To analyze usage trends to improve the App and develop new features.
  • To personalize your App experience and offer you more relevant content.
  • To provide you with technical support and respond to your inquiries.
  • To send App updates, security notifications, and support messages.
  • To ensure security, prevent fraud, and enforce our App Terms of Use.
  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to legal requests.
  • To process User-Generated Content through AI models for the purpose of video creation.

How We Share Your Information

We do not share or sell your information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • **With Service Providers:** We may work with third-party service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics, payment processors, AI models) to support the App's functionality. These providers may access your information only as necessary to perform their services and are subject to contractual obligations to protect your privacy.
    • Specifically, the text and media inputs you provide for video creation may be sent to third-party services providing AI models (e.g., OpenAI, Google AI, etc.) for processing. These models process your inputs to generate your video.
  • **For Legal Obligations:** We may disclose your information as required by law, such as in response to a legal process, government request, or copyright infringement claim.
  • **Business Transfers:** In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. In such a case, we will notify you that your personal information will be subject to a different privacy policy.
  • **With Your Consent:** We will obtain your explicit consent before sharing your information for any other purpose.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. We may need to retain and use your information to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Videos you create and associated inputs may be stored on our servers to provide the service. You can delete your content from your account settings within the App if you wish. When content is deleted, it is removed from our active systems within a commercially reasonable timeframe; however, it may remain in backup copies.

Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical security measures designed to protect the security of your information. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

Your Privacy Rights

You may have certain rights regarding your data. These may include:

  • **Right to Access:** The right to request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • **Right to Rectification:** The right to request the correction of any incomplete or inaccurate personal data we hold about you.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact information at the end of this Policy. We will respond to your request in accordance with applicable laws.
